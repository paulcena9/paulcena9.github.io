Passion for Craftsmanship

Bachman Woodworking is owned and operated by Jon Bachman, a skilled and passionate woodworker. Specializing in handcrafted beds, tables, chairs, cutting boards, and custom projects, Jon creates functional and beautiful pieces for friends, family, and beyond.

This website serves as a platform to showcase his work and share insights into his woodworking journey through a blog.
